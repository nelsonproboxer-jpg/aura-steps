The Size Atelier

Find Your Perfect Fit

Three minutes with a sheet of paper and a pencil is all it takes to know your Aura Steps size for life.

1 · Trace

Stand barefoot on a sheet of paper in the evening (feet are largest then) and trace your foot's outline.

2 · Measure

Measure from the back of the heel to the tip of your longest toe, in centimetres. Measure both feet — use the larger.

3 · Match

Find your measurement in the chart below. Between sizes? We recommend sizing up for pointed silhouettes.

Narrow feet?

Our slingbacks and pointed silhouettes run slightly narrow-friendly. Take your true size.

Wide feet?

Choose our block heels, mules or flats and go half a size up for all-day comfort.

Still unsure?
